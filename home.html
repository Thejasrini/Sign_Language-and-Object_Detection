About InvisiConnect

InvisiConnect is a web-based application designed to bridge the gap for differently-abled individuals, especially those with visual and hearing impairments. This platform allows users to detect and recognize sign language gestures and common objects with the help of real-time computer vision and machine learning. Our goal is to make communication and interaction more accessible, allowing visually impaired users to navigate their surroundings and communicate with others through sign language recognition.

Our Mission

To enhance accessibility and communication for differently-abled individuals through innovative technology.
