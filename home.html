About InvisiConnect

InvisiConnect is a web-based application designed to bridge the gap for differently-abled individuals, especially those with visual and hearing impairments. It enables real-time recognition of sign language gestures and common objects using advanced machine learning and computer vision techniques. My goal is to enhance communication and accessibility for all users.
